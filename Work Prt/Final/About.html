ABOUT US

What is yourvoteplus?

YourVoteplus is a digital platform to help simplify the process of voting for South Africans by providing a remote voting platform to vote from the comfort of theirs homes or on the go.

What is our goal?

We aim to save alot of South Africans time and money thats is used by means of getting to their nearest voting stations to exercise their right to vote
